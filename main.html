Here are guidelines for playing the game:

1. Choose your preferred game type: 8 images, 10 images, or 12 images.
2. Once selected, the game will begin.
3. For the 8 images game, you have 120 seconds to solve the puzzle.
4. If you opt for the 10 images game, you'll be given 150 seconds to complete the puzzle.
5. The 12 images game allows you 180 seconds to finish the puzzle.
6. Your objective is to match each picture with its identical counterpart. Click on two images to reveal them, and wait for them to disappear before attempting the next pair.
7. If the timer runs out, simply reload the page to restart the game.
